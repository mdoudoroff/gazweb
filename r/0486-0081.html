101 Best New Cocktails

Pernelle

1 rosemary sprig

1 barspoon agave syrup

60 ml U’Luvka vodka

15 ml pear eau de vie (poire williams)

30 ml St. Germain

30 ml fresh lemon juice

Club soda

1 rosemary sprig, as garnish

1 lemon twist, as garnish

Muddle the rosemary and syrup in a mixing glass. Add the remaining ingredients except soda. Shake without ice and strain into a highball glass filled with crushed ice. Add the soda and garnishes.

Adapted from a recipe by Colin Asare-Appiah, New York City.

“Pernelle was the wife of Hermes the Alchemist. Behind every great man is a great woman, and this cocktail is a nod to them.”—Colin Asare-Appiah
