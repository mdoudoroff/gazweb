Negroni’s Loss

30 ml Bols Genever

22.5 ml Gran Classico

22.5 ml Carpano Antica Formula

1 lemon twist, as garnish

1 orange twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a glass over fresh ice. Add the garnishes.

Adapted from a recipe by Jason Schiffer, 320 Main, Seal Beach, CA.

gaz sez: This is one weird mother of a drink—it’s all over the place on the palate. But I kept going back for more. Drinking this was like reading Umberto Eco’s Foucault’s Pendulum. I didn’t really understand it, but it was so intriguing that I had to keep going to the very end.
