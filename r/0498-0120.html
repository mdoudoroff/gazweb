101 Best New Cocktails

Topsy Turvey

60 ml Crop Organic Tomato Vodka

30 ml tarragon syrup

15 ml fresh lemon juice

3 grape tomatoes, skewered, as garnish

Shake hard over ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Mathew Giblin, 42 The Restaurant, White Plains, NY.

gaz sez: When you get to be my age (60) you start to think that your taste buds might not be what they used to be, but this Crop tomato vodka proves that it’s just ain’t so. It tastes exactly like the fresh-off-the-vine tomatoes tasted when I was a kid and we bought them from local producers near Kirkham in Lancashire. Mathew did a very fine job with this drink. The taste of summertime in a glass, it is.
