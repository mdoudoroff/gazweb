Osaka Smash

3 Lemon wedges

3 Shiso leaves

1 small piece of ginger

45 ml Suntory Yamazaki 12 Years Old

30 ml Domain De Canton Ginger Liqueur

Methodology: In a mixing glass, muddle lemon wedges, shiso leaves and ginger. Add the Yamazaki and Canton. Shake and strain into an ice filled rocks glass. Candied ginger garnish.

Ben Hammer, The Backhouse, Los Angeles, CA, USA

”A variation on the whiskey smash. I have featured this drink at two different bars that I worked at, and it was very popular. I chose the name because Osaka, Japan is where Yamazaki [whisky] originates.”—Ben Hammer
