Gunpowder Green Tea Infused Honey

Combine in a sauce pan:

10 oz water

Heat to just below a boil.

Add:

2 tbsp gunpowder green tea leaves

Steep five minutes.

Add:

10 oz honey

Stir to dissolve.

Cool, bottle and refrigerate.

Adapted from a recipe by Donnie Pratt, Cucina 24, Asheville, North Carolina
