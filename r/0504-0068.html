Swizzle from the Barrel

45 ml Nikka Whisky From The Barrel

15 ml Carpano Antica Formula

10 ml Rodenbach Grand Cru Beer Syrup

20 ml fresh orange juice

10 ml fresh lemon juice

1 barspoon of gum syrup

Pour all ingredients in big cognac snifter, fill up with crushed ice and start swizzling and serve with a smile.

Jesse den Dulk, Jigger’s The Noble Drugstore, Gent, Belgium

“The Swizzle From the Barrel brings together Nikka Whisky From the Barrel, a rich yet smooth Japanese aged blend, and Rodenbach Grand Cru, a Belgian beer. It was my winning entry for the Nikka Perfect Serve 2013.

“Rodenbach is a fruity ale aged in oak barrels with a wine-like taste. It blends well with the Nikka, hitting similar notes, whilst adding some freshness and acidity. The Nikka from the Barrel works great in cocktails due to its complexity and high alcohol content (51.4% ABV) but yet smooth character. The resulting concoction is a big, swizzled, fruity drink, through which the character of the whisky still stands out. If you can’t get your hands on Rodenbach, you can always experiment with other beers with dark, fruity, malt and wood notes.

“I chose to swizzle this drink, which chills it quickly without little dilution and hence extra whisky flavor. The best thing about swizzling however is that it always puts a smile on people’s faces.”—Jesse den Dulk.
