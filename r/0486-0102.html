101 Best New Cocktails

Smoking Aces

75 ml Don Modesto añejo tequila

30 ml pure maple syrup

1 dash liquid smoke

1 lemon twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Flame the lemon twist over the drink, then add as garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Duane Fernandez, Jr., Entwine, New York City.

“I wanted to create a classic-style cocktail with a fantastic aged tequila…”—Duane Fernandez, Jr.

gaz sez: I usually hate maple syrup in cocktails, and although this isn’t the only drink that calls for it that I’ve liked, it’s one of a very few. The liquid smoke is the ingredient that compensates for the maple sweetness here, and it adds dimension to the cocktail, too. Nicely done, Duane.
