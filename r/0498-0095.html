Rabbit’s Dilemma

45 ml Templeton rye

45 ml Bolthouse Farms carrot juice

22.5 ml Butternut Squash Simple Syrup

15 ml House-made Cinnamon Vodka

15 ml fresh lemon juice

3 dashes (or 1 barspoon) fresh lime juice

Shake over ice and strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.

Adapted from a recipe by Frank Caiafa, The Vault at Pfaff’s, New York City.

gaz sez: Frank Caiafa doesn’t have a style all his own—his drinks bounce around all over the place, and lots of his creations are pretty much spot on. This one’s no exception. At first glance I didn’t think that this drink was for me, but the rye pulls everything together and makes this one a pretty serious quaff. Who’d have thunk it?
