Cinnamon Tincture

Tightly pack a mason jar with quality cinnamon bark (not stick, as they are steamed before they are rolled and lose some intensity). Top up with high-proof vodka or grain alcohol and let sit for 6 weeks. Strain through a coffee filter and bottle. This is a very versatile tincture that tends to come in handy.

Adapted from a recipe by Wes Galloway, Black Beans Steakhouse and Lounge, Port Hope, Canada.
