Pooja du Pina

4 fresh pineapple cubes about 1 cm square

1 barspoon dried lavender flowers

30 ml Lemon Hart 151 Demerara rum (or other overproof rum)

30 ml Appleton Estate 12 Year Old rum (or other aged Jamaican rum)

30 ml Havana Club Añejo 3 Años rum (or other Cuban rum or Puerto Rican rum)

30 ml fresh lemon juice

30 ml agave syrup

1 dash Angostura bitters

Pineapple leaves, as garnish

Fresh lavender, as garnish

Muddle the pineapple and lavender in a mixing glass. Add ice and the remaining ingredients. Shake very very hard and strain into a tiki glass. Garnish extensively with pineapple leaves and fresh lavender.

Adapted from a recipe by Oron Lerner, Mapal, Haifa, Israel.
