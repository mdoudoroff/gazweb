Coffee-Infused Gran Classico

Combine:

160 g whole bean medium-roast coffee

750 ml Gran Classico

Macerate 24 hours. Use quality coffee like Intelligentsia or another local proper roaster. None of that dark roast rubbish! Whole bean only. Strain through cheesecloth into a clean bottle.

Adapted from a recipe by Laura Lindsay, Providence Restaurant, Los Angeles, CA.
