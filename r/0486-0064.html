101 Best New Cocktails

Martilla

1 barspoon lemon zest

45 ml Don Julio añejo tequila

3 barspoons Luxardo maraschino liqueur

30 ml Noilly Prat dry vermouth

1 lemon twist, as garnish

Put the lemon zest in a mixing glass and fill it with cubed ice. Add the remaining ingredients and stir for 10 seconds depending on the ice. Double-strain into a chilled cocktail glass and add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Bas Verhoeven, Door 74, Amsterdam.

“This real smooth drink is my signature drink and one of the best-selling cocktails at Door 74 in Amsterdam. You will take journey and taste every flavor step-by-step, and the tequila is the last taste you would expect to find in this sublime drink.”—Bas Verhoeven
