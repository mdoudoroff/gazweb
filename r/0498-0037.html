Gaz’s Birthday Bitters

Combine in a jar:

40 cl Bacardi 151

10 cl Pampero Blanco

15 g dried orange zest

11.2 g calamus root

11.2 g burdock root

11.5 g oak bark

7.6 g cardamom pods

2 barspoons of Homemade Tonic Syrup

Macerate in a sealed jar for 2 weeks, shaking daily. Filter, add 500 ml water to the residue and filter that. Blend the two and bottle it.

Adapted from a recipe by Timon Salomez, Gent, Belgium.
