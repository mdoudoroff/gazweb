Coconut Rose Foam

Bloom 2 gelatin sheets in ice water (soak until soft) for about ten minutes.

Combine:

9 cl Monin Rose Syrup

6 cl lemon juice

6 cl water

9 cl simple syrup

Fine strain into a saucepan.

Remove gelatin from ice water, squeeze out excess water, and add to saucepan.

Heat saucepan over medium-high heat just until gelatin is dissolved. Immediately remove the saucepan from the heat. Do not boil.

Add:

24 cl unsweetened coconut milk (canned)

Cool ten minutes.

Pour mixture into a NO2 charger. Charge with two NO2 charger cartridges and immediately shake well. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight. Shake well upside down before discharging foam onto cocktail. Store in refrigerator for up to 4 to 5 days. Makes 1 canister of foam.

Adapted from a recipe by Kathy Casey, Liquid Kitchen, Seattle.
