Chamomile and Jasmine Infused Dry Vermouth

Combine:

750-ml bottle Dolin dry vermouth

2 teaspoons chamomile tea leaves

2 teaspoons jasmine tea leaves

Infuse stand for 1.5 to 2 hours, tasting while infusing to make sure the tea doesn’t overpower the vermouth too much.

Strain, bottle, and keep in the fridge for up to 2 months.

Adapted from a recipe by Grant Dingwall, Corner Bar, Auckland, New Zealand.

“A martini-styled drink named after the Egyptian goddess who was worshipped as the ideal mother and wife, also the patron of nature and magic.”—Grant Dingwall
