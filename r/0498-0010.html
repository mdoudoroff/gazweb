Bacardi Patrimonio

1 slice of pineapple (about ½-inch, preferably fresh Victoria)

10 ml maple syrup

10 ml fresh lime juice

30 ml Bacardi Superior

10 ml Bacardi 8 Años

20 ml Martini Gold

1 dash Fee Brothers cherry bitters

½ barspoon orange blossom water

1 small mint sprig, as garnish

Muddle the pineapple, syrup, and lime juice in a mixing glass. Add ice and the remaining ingredients. Shake and double-strain in a chilled cocktail glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Anthony Berg, Le Hussard, Nice, France.

“This drink was created for the 2012 Bacardi Legacy for the 150th anniversary of the brand. The mix of the Bacardi Superior and Bacardi 8 is intended to create a kind of “solera” in a cocktail. The pineapple brings some fruitiness, Martini gold gives his spicy side, and cherry bitters and orange blossom water leave a nice aftertaste. After tasting the mix shows some hints of cinnamon. Enjoy.” Anthony Berg.
