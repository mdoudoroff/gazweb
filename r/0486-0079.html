Parisian Barmaid

60 ml Martell VS cognac

15 ml Rothman & Winter Orchard Apricot

22.5 ml fresh lemon juice

15 ml simple syrup

2 dashes Angostura bitters

1 large lemon twist, as garnish

Shake over ice and strain into a large chilled coupe. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe for Valentine’s Day by Erick Castro, The Rickhouse, San Francisco.

“Tread lightly, for this busty treasure is every bit as alluring and rambunctious as her name suggests. Flirtatious yet delightful, this playful beverage is ideal for anyone who does not believe that style and substance should remain mutually exclusive.”— Rickhouse Cocktail Menu, February, 2010
