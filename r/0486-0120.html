101 Best New Cocktails

The Violent Bear

60 ml Bols Genever

15 ml Punt e Mes

15 ml Zirbenz

2 dashes Bittermens Xocolatl Mole bitters

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled coupe.

Adapted from a recipe by Jeremy Swift, The JakeWalk, Brooklyn, NY.

“This is a serious drink for before dinner to open the appetite or for after to settle the tummy. Don’t let the Violent Bear scare you; in the end it’s really well balanced.”—Jeremy Swift

gaz sez: Not many people can work well with Zirbenz Stone Pine Liqueur, but Jeremy pulls it off beautifully in the Violent Bear. The combination of the Bols Genever and the Punt e Mes works as a wall that simply blocks the Zirbenz from dominating the drink, but nevertheless, they let it stand up and be counted. This is a very well thought-out drink.
