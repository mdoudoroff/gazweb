Apple-Spice Infused Dubonnet

Crush in a mortar:

1 tsp allspice berries

1 tsp coriander seeds

Pour into a jar.

Add:

3 apple peels

2 tsp lemon zest

2 cinnamon sticks

1 liter Dubonnet

Infuse five days. Filter.

Adapted from a recipe by Joseph Boley, Red House, Paris
