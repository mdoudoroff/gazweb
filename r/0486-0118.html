Torch’n Scorch

60 ml light rum (Banks 5 Island Rum works well)

30 ml cream of coconut

15 ml fresh lime juice

15 ml honey

½ teaspoon chipotle purée

10 basil leaves

2 sprigs cilantro (leaves removed from stem)

1 cup crushed ice

1 cherry pepper, as garnish

Place everything in a blender and process on high until smooth. Pour into a margarita glass and add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Payman Bahmani, Life’s a Cocktail, Brooklyn, NY.

“Hopefully this drink shows that blended drinks, even if not taken seriously, can still be seriously good.”—Payman Bahmani

gaz sez: This is a fabulous drink, and I love the fact that Payman simply threw everything into a blender instead of muddling and shaking and straining, etc. The crowning glory, for me, is the chipotle puree—I dashed to the supermarket to buy a can of chipotles en adobo so I could try this drink, and now I keep chipotle sauce in my fridge at all times—it comes in handy in the kitchen, as well as behind the bar.
