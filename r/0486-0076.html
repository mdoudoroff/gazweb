Ektoras Binikos’ Shiso Syrup

Combine in a sauce pan:

150 g shiso leaves

1500 g (7.5 cups) sugar

1.5 liters (6.25 cups) water

¾ tablespoon salt

Bring to a boil, then remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.

Refrigerate, then strain the next day. This will keep for a month.

Adapted from a recipe by Ektoras Binikos, Oceana, New York City.
