101 Best New Cocktails

Speakeasy

1 sugar cube

70 ml Ardbeg Ten Years Old scotch whisky

1 barspoon Galliano L’Autentico

3 drops The Bitter Truth Old Time Aromatic bitters

3 drops The Bitter Truth Chocolate bitters

Pernod, as rinse

Crush the sugar cube in a mixing glass. Add ice and the remaining ingredients. Stir and strain into a chilled Pernod-rinsed old-fashioned glass.

Adapted from a recipe by Oscar Quagliarini, Juleps New York Bar, Milan.

“A twist on the Sazerac.”—Oscar Quagliarini
