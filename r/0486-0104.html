Lynette Marerro’s Smoked Demerara

Combine in a saucepan:

1 tablespoon lapsong souchong tea leaves

24 cl boiling water

Steep for 2 hours.

Add:

200 g (1 cup) demerara sugar

Simmer and reduce.

Cool, strain, and bottle.

Adapted from a recipe by Lynette Marerro, Peels and DrinksAt6, New York City.
