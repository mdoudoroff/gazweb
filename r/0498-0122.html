Transatlantic Connection

50 ml Calle 23 blanco tequila

20 ml Cherry Heering

20 ml fresh lime juice

10 ml simple syrup

1 fresh vanilla pod, split and seeds loosened

1 orange twist, as garnish

Shake vigorously over ice and double-strain into chilled coupette. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Stefano Censi, Geist, Copenhagen.

“This drink was inspired by an afternoon with Tomas Estes and Sophie Decobecq speaking about the wonders of tequila. The Transatlantic Connection was inspired about the good times between elements from Mexico and Denmark, hence the tequila and Cherry Heering.” Stefano Censi.

gaz sez: Any afternoon spent with Sophie and Tomas is an afternoon well spent—the vanilla in this drink is an inspired stroke, and the Calle 23 is such a fine base to use.
