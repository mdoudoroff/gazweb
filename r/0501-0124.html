Oolong Green Tea Raspberry Syrup

Combine in a saucepan:

8 oz boiling water

4 grams oolong green tea leaves

Infuse three minutes. Strain.

Add:

8 oz fresh raspberries

Cook on low heat until the raspberries dissipate.

Remove from heat.

Add:

8 oz superfine sugar

Stir to dissolve.

Strain through a double layer of dampened cheesecloth. Store in the refrigerator.

Adapted from a recipe by Nick Caputo, The Priory Tavern, London
