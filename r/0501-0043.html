Chad Larson’s Spiced Tea Syrup

Combine in a saucepan:

18 cl boiling water

1 teabag of Republic of Tea Cardamom Cinnamon

Steep for 30 minutes.

Add:

200 g demerara sugar

Heat and stir until sugar dissolves. Cool to room temperature and store in the refrigerator.

Adapted from a recipe by Chad Larson, Cafe Maude at Loring, Minneapolis, MN.
