Strega’s Walnut Manhattan

1 lemon twist

45 ml Old Potrero rye whiskey

15 ml Carpano Antica Formula vermouth

7.58 ml Charbay Black Walnut liqueur (add a dash more)

7.5 ml Strega

Rub the yellow side of the lemon twist over the interior of a chilled cocktail glass, then discard the twist. Stir the remaining ingredients over ice and strain the drink into the prepared glass.

Adapted from a recipe by Victoria D’Amato-Moran, Cent’Anni Cocktails, San Francisco.

gaz sez: I’ve known Victoria for quite a few years, and I’ve ceased being surprised that her wacky formulas work so well, although the ingredients in this drink aren’t quite as strange as those found in some of her other creations.

For me it’s the Charbay Black Walnut liqueur that brings this to life, though I must say that the vanilla notes that the Strega brings to the party give the drink more depth, too. The brilliance in this one, though, lies in the ratio of Old Potrero rye to Carpano Antica Formula vermouth. The Carpano can take charge of a drink if it isn’t used judiciously, and Victoria hit the nail on the head when she formulated this one.
