Oye Mi Canto

30 ml Tamarind-Infused Tequila

60 ml Martini Rosato

5 ml Del Maguey Chichicapa

1 dash Bob’s Bitters Orange & Mandarin

1 apple blossom, as garnish

1 jasmine flower, as garnish

Stir over ice with a barspoon, or gaz-style, with finger is suitable:). Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and add the garnishes.

Adapted from a recipe by Alex Kratena, Artesian, The Langham Hotel, London, UK.

“Delight in the glass and very complex aperitif! Notes of young tequila and tamarind with pomegranate and raspberries, hint of spice and light smoky notes in background with extensively long finish! Winner of Tapatio Competition 2011 ( London, UK). “Alex Kratena.
