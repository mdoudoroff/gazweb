101 Best New Cocktails

Citrine

30 ml dry gin (Half Moon Orchard Gin, Sipsmith London Dry Gin, or Uncle Val’s Botanical Gin if you’re feeling freaky)

30 ml Vya Sweet Vermouth or Dolin Rouge

30 ml Suze

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Jake says that he usually skips the garnish, “but a lemon twist would probably be lovely”.

Jake Sutton, Stay-at-Home Bartender, Westminster, CO, USA
