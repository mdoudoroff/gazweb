101 Best New Cocktails

Grasshopper

1 ½ ounces green crème de menthe

1 ½ ounces white crème de cacao

1 ounce half-and-half

1 teaspoon Fernet Branca

1 pinch sea salt

4 ounces vanilla ice cream

8 ounces crushed ice, prepared with a Lewis bag and mallet, a tea towel and rolling pin, or a food processor

Mint sprig for garnish

Combine all ingredients except the mint sprig in a blender, and blend on high speed until smooth. Serve in a tall, frozen glass, and garnish with a mint sprig.

Jeffrey Morgenthaler, Clyde Common, Portland, Oregon, USA.

“We wanted to pay homage to the classic Midwestern style of ice cream drink, so we toyed around with the recipe until we got it right. The Fernet Branca added a whole lot more complexity, but brought unwanted bitterness to the party, so that’s how we ended up with a pinch of sea salt,” Jeffrey Morgenthaler
