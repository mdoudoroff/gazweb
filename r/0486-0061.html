Ian Storror’s Spiced Sugar Syrup

Combine in a sauce pan:

72 cl water

300 g (1.5 cups) brown sugar

300 g (1.5 cups) demerara sugar

1 cinnamon stick

5 cloves

1 slice of ginger (~1 inch, peeled)

3 cardomom pods

1 star anise

Warm gently until the sugar dissolves, 15 minutes tops.

Cool, strain the spices, and bottle.

Adapted from a recipe by Ian Storror, Future Inns, Bristol, England.
