101 Best New Cocktails

Southern Baptist

60 ml Michter’s US-1 Single Barrel Straight Rye

22.5 ml fresh lime juice

22.5 ml ginger syrup

1 lime wedge, as garnish

Shake vigorously over ice and double-strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Sother Teague, Amor Y Amargo, New York City.

“This drink was inspired by the classic Presbyterian, altered to reveal a southern sensibility. Cheers!” Sother Teague.
