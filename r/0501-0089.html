101 Best New Cocktails

English Bishop

Stud an orange with about 40 cloves and bake at 205 degrees C (400 degrees F) until golden brown. Quarter the orange and place in a saucepan with 750 ml of port. Simmer for 20 minutes and then add about 200 g (1 cup) of sugar. Cool and bottle.

Adapted from a recipe by Daniel Brancusi, Vitae, New York City.
