Punch de Chevalier

25 ml Remy Martin VSOP cognac

20 ml green Chartreuse

15 ml Gabriel Boudier crème de pêche

30 ml fresh lemon juice

10 ml sugar syrup

2 lemon wedges, as garnish

Shake over ice and strain into a highball glass over fresh ice. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Merlin Jerebine, 1806, Melbourne, Australia.

“I came up with this drink when playing around with the punch family. I like the punchy herbal notes of this drink. I entered The Suntory Cup, a cocktail comp in Australia, and won with this drink.”—Merlin Jerebine
