Roman Milostivy’s Chamomile & Cinnamon Syrup

Combine in a sauce pan:

1 l hot water

3-5 chamomile tea bags

Infuse 5-7 minutes and strain.

Add:

600 g (3 cups) superfine sugar or brown sugar

10 cinnamon sticks.

Stir to dissolve.

Simmer 45-60 minutes. (Do not boil.)

Remove from heat and return to room temperature.

Filter and bottle.

Adapted from a recipe by Roman Milostivy, The United Bartenders, Moscow.
