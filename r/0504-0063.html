101 Best New Cocktails

Slow Learner

30 ml rye whiskey

30 ml cognac

15 ml Averna

15 ml red vermouth

1 dash Bittermens Hopped Grapefruit Bitters

1 lemon twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into an ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Add the garnish.
