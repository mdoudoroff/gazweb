Oaxacan Spice

45 ml Don Julio añejo tequila

15 ml Los Amantes Joven

15 ml fresh lime juice

7.5 ml Campari

7.5 ml simple syrup (1:1 Sugar in the Raw)

7.5 ml St. Elizabeth Allspice Dram

1 orange twist, as garnish

Shake over ice and fine-strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Michael Glassberg, Domenica Restaurant, New Orleans, LA.

“I really wanted to order some mezcal so I needed to create a drink that could work on an Italian menu. There’s a lot going on in this one but as my wife just said, ‘I can’t imagine gaz not liking it.’” Michael Glassberg.

gaz sez: Here’s a nice use of the Allspice Dram, and although there’s just 7.5 ml, or ¼ ounce, of it in this drink, it plays a pretty significant role and helps bind the other ingredients together. Lots of people use the Allspice Dram in rum-based drinks, but I’ve not seen it used too often with tequila. Nice thinking, Michael.
