101 Best New Cocktails

Queen Eleanor

45 ml gin

15 ml dry vermouth

15 ml crème de pêche

1 dash The Bitter Truth Original Celery bitters

1 lemon twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Brandon Josie, 15 Romolo, San Francisco.

“From Queen Consort in France to Queen of England, Eleanor of Aquitaine was worth talking about. So take a trip from France to England with this twist on a gin martini… It’ll be worth talking about.”—Brandon Josie
