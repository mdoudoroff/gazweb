101 Best New Cocktails

Vice President

45 ml Havana Club Añejo Especial

30 ml Mandarine Napoleon

15 ml Campari

First of all start cooling a cocktail glass by stuffing it with ice then pour the ingredients into a mixing glass full of ice and stir until everything is cold. Pour it into the cocktail glass and there you go.

Adapted from a recipe by Ludovico De Biaggi, Gran Bar Danzon, Buenos Aires.

“This hasn’t got a garnish ‘cause it doesn’t need one. Hope you like it. I have tried it with customers from all over the world and in 99.9% of the chances they love it. Thank you for this opportunity.” Ludovico De Biaggi.
