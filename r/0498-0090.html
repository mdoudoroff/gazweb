Perfect Summit Manhattan

75 ml Woodford Reserve bourbon

22.5 ml Vya dry vermouth

22.5 ml Muscat (Chambers preferred)

7.5 ml orange curaçao

3 drops Fee Brothers Whiskey Barrel Aged bitters

1 orange twist, as garnish

1 maraschino cherry, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Add the garnishes.

Adapted from a recipe by Pilar Zeglin, Summit Restaurant Bar/Midwest Culinary Institute, Cincinnati, OH.

“This cocktail is requested and sought after since its inception. This cocktail is very well balanced and has a way of creeping up on you!” Pilar Zeglin.

gaz sez: Pilar pulls this one together very nicely indeed. Every ingredient makes itself known, and the curacao—I used Pierre Ferrand—plays very pretty with the bitters. Another “keep it simple” recipe that reaches for the heights, and gets there, too.
