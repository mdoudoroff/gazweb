Sesame Sugar Syrup

Combine 2 kg (2.2 lb) sugar, 1L (1 qt) water, 1 kg (2.2 lb) white sesame seeds, and a slice of ginger in a saucepan. Set over medium-low heat and stir till the sugar dissolves; donâ€™t boil or crush the sesame seeds. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from heat, let cool, then cover and set aside for two days. Fine-strain and add two shots of overproof rum.

Adapted from a recipe by Tess Posthumus, Door 74, Amsterdam.
