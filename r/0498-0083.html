101 Best New Cocktails

Old Boy

45 ml Germain-Robin Craft-Method Brandy

45 ml Old Overholt rye whiskey

15 ml Old-Fashioned Rum Syrup

1 orange twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a nice big double old-fashioned glass over a couple of nice big rocks of ice. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Brent Butler, Blackbird, San Francisco, CA.

“It’s just a really nice Old-Fashioned.” Brent Butler.

gaz sez: This one’s right up my alley, and the old-fashioned rum syrup is a masterpiece. I confess to not using the correct rum when I made this syrup, but the Zacapa 23 works very well indeed here.
