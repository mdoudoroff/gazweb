101 Best New Cocktails

Julius Orange

60 ml Pierre Ferrand Dry Curaçao

15 ml Cruzan Single Barrel rum

15 ml lemon juice

15 ml heavy cream

½ tsp vanilla syrup

½ tsp Martinique cane syrup

1 dash Regan’s orange bitters

freshly grated nutmeg, as garnish

Build all ingredients in shaker, adding cream last. If possible, shake with a large block of ice for maximum aeration. Strain into a double rocks/old fashioned glass over crushed ice. Add the garnish.

Brad Farran, Clover Club, Death&Co., the Shanty, New York, USA. Photo by F. Martin Ramin for The Wall Street Journal, Styling by Anne Cardenas.
