Subterranean Swizzle

30 ml Appleton Reserve rum

30 ml Osocalis Rare Alambic Brandy

15 ml pineapple juice

15 ml Trader Tiki’s Cinnamon syrup

15 ml lemon juice

1 barspoon demerara syrup (1:1)

4-5 dashes Improved Bitters, as garnish

1 mint sprig, as garnish

1 pineapple slice, as garnish

Put all the ingredients except the bitters in a collins glass and fill with crushed ice. Using a swizzle stick, swizzle for 45 seconds, making sure the glass is nice and frosty on the outside and that all the ingredients are well mixed. Top with more crushed ice. Dash liberally the Improved Bitters over the ice until the top is entirely covered. Add the garnishes.

Adapted from a recipe by Brian MacGregor, Jasper’s Corner Tap, San Francisco, CA.

“This was a drink I originally came up while doing a guest bartender spot at the Needle in Thread, within Tavern Law, in Seattle. I recall serving it to the always generous Paul Clark. I later refined it for the Appleteon Re-Mixology contest, where it was chosen for the semi-finals here in SF. Sadly I did not win said contest, but I had fun nonetheless. This little ditty is refreshing and complex. With a bold rich flavor of all the ingredients coming through with a burst of flavor right out the gate, yet has a beautiful finish as the aromatic blend of the bitters come through in the end. Hope you enjoy it as much as I do.” Brian MacGregor.
