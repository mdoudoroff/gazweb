The Cure

60 ml Herradura Añejo

22.5 ml fresh lemon juice

15 ml honey syrup

15 ml Domain de Canton ginger liqueur

7.5 ml el Silencio Mezcal

1 lemon twist, as garnish

Shake all ingredients except the mezcal over ice and strain into an ice filled double old-fashioned glass. Float the mezcal on top. Add the garnish.

Eric Tecosky, Jones Hollywood, West Hollywood, CA, USA
