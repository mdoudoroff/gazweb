Matt Peckham’s Bianco Mint Foam

Muddle in a mixing glass:

20 to 24 mint leaves

25 ml Monin Miel

Add:

1 egg white

50 ml Martini & Rossi Bianco vermouth

25 ml fresh lemon juice

Shake hard.

Fine-strain into a whipped cream canister.

Charge with 2 nitrogen cartridges.

Adapted from a recipe by Matt Peckham, Liquidchefs Cocktail Lounge, Johannesburg, South Africa.
