Diana Haider’s Japanese Syrup

Combine in a sauce pan:

3 chrysanthemum tea bags

3 jasmine tea bags

8 oz boiling water

Steep three minutes and remove tea bags.

Add:

8 oz superfine sugar

zest of 2 limes

Simmer and stir until the sugar is dissolved.

Remove from heat and infuse for three hours.

Strain and bottle.
