Bitter Stripper

40 ml Plymouth Gin

15 ml Dolin Blanc

10 ml Salers Gentiane

5 ml Cointreau

1 orange twist, as garnish

Stir all ingredients over ice, and strain into a chilled martini glass. Zest the orange peel over the glass, twist and balance on the rim as the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Dee Allen, 399, Perth, Australia

“This drink is based on the negroni. The intention was to replicate the flavours of a negroni without using campari or a sweet vermouth, to create a clear drink.”—Dee Allen
