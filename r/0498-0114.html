Stirring Up The Kraken

65 ml The Kraken Black Spiced Rum

20 ml sugar syrup

4 dashes Angostura bitters

25 ml apple juice

1 orange twist, as garnish

1 maraschino cherry, as garnish

Stir rum, syrup, and bitters over cubed ice in a rocks glass. Crown the mix with the apple juice and add the garnishes.

“A spiced rum twist on a Treacle recipe. The vanilla notes and smooth flavours of the Kraken rum are amplified by the bitters and the apple. My stab at doing something interesting with a great product, wasted on endless Dark and Stormys!” Dave Thompson.
