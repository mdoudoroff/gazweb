Roast Chestnut Tincture

Steep 15 chestnuts in 240 ml (1 cup) Appletonâ€™s 8 year rum for two weeks, agitating once a day. Strain through a cheesecloth, then add a pinch of saffron and a small handful of cassia bark. Leave overnight, then strain again and add a dash of maple syrup. Bottle and keep in the refrigerator.
