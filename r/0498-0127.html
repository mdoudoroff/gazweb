101 Best New Cocktails

What is Hip

1 barspoon pink peppercorns

30 ml strong brewed rosehip tea

40 ml Tanqueray gin

10 ml fresh lemon juice

1 dash Cherry Heering

10 to 15 ml Gomme Syrup

Muddle the peppercorns and rosehip tea in a shaker. Add ice and the remaining ingredients. Shake vigorously before double-straining into a chilled cocktail glass.

Adapted from a recipe by Monica Berg, Icebar Oslo, Oslo, Norway.

“The name is from a song by the SF based Tower of Power –What is Hip. I heard them live in New Orleans couple of years ago and absolutely loved it! I think the lyrics are very fitting because when I made this drink first, gin was very much out of style in Norway and rum-based fruit drinks very much in.” Monica Berg.
