101 Best New Cocktails

Teenage Riot

45 ml Rittenhouse rye

45 ml Cynar

15 ml Dolin dry vermouth

15 ml Lustau dry amontillado sherry

2 dashes orange bitters

1 lemon twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Squeeze the twist over the drink, then add as garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Tonia Guffey, Dram, Flatiron Lounge, and Lani Kai, New York City.
