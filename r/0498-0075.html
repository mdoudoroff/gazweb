Missing Link Aviation

60 ml gin

22.5 ml maraschino liqueur

22.5 ml fresh lemon juice

Crème de Violette Sugar

Shake over ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass rimmed with crème de violette sugar.

Adapted from a recipe by Camper English, San Francisco, CA.

“For something like 60 years, the Aviation recipe was copied incorrectly from book to book as only gin, maraschino liqueur, and lemon juice, even through the beginning of the current classic cocktail revival. Then someone figured out the creme de violette, which turned the drink sky-blue and gave the cocktail its name, was missing from the original recipe.   “I thought a fun drink would be a missing link between the wrong and right recipes, leaving the drinker the option to have it either way by licking the creme de violette off the rim before sipping or just enjoying what’s in the glass on its own.” Camper English.
