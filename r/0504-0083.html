Chamomile-infused Pierre Ferrand Ambre Cognac

Combine:

75 cl Pierre Ferrand Cognac Ambre

5 grams dried chamomile (about 8 heaping barspoons)

Infuse 30 minutes, to taste (until chamomile flavor is just coming through).

Strain through a Super Bag and bottle.

Refrigerate.

Alex Day, Nitecap, New York, USA
