Moroccan Punch

45 ml gin

22.5 ml orgeat syrup

15 ml Hum liqueur

30 ml fresh pineapple juice

15 ml fresh lemon juice

1 flag, as garnish

Shake over ice and strain into a double old-fashioned glass over fresh ice. Add the garnish (use an orchid and infused cherry if available).

Adapted from a recipe by Thomas Kunick, Blackhawk Country Club, Madison, WI.

“House-made orgeat syrup is ideal for this drink, however Taylor’s will do in a pinch. Also, dressing it up Tiki style would be very appropriate. Thank you for consideration.” Thomas Kunick.

gaz sez: It’s the Hum liqueur that makes this one stand out—the ginger and spices in it make great foils for the orgeat, and the gin makes an ideal platform. It’s a tiki-style drink that I’d order more than once.
