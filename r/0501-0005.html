101 Best New Cocktails

Bases Clearing Double

60 ml Gingerbread Rooibus-Infused Bulleit Rye

22 ml orgeat

22 ml fresh orange juice

22 ml fresh lime juice

22 ml pineapple juice

grated nutmeg, as garnish

Shake over ice and strain into a crushed-ice-filled collins glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Sandy Levine, The Oakland, Ferndale, MI
