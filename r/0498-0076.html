Crème de Violette Sugar

Pour about 60 ml of Rothman & Winter Crème de Violette in a silicone cupcake cup, silicone baking pan, or Silpat mat. Place in oven at a low setting, about 170°F, and leave overnight to dehydrate. You may have to crack the surface of the liqueur every now and then to allow the liquid beneath it to dehydrate completely. After drying, grind the crystallized crème de violette in a mortar and pestle or a spice grinder into a fine dust.

Adapted from a recipe by Camper English, San Francisco, CA.
