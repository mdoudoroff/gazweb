101 Best New Cocktails

Andean Dusk

Muddle in a mixing glass:

4 red grapes

Add:

15 ml fresh lemon juice

15 ml simple syrup

30 ml La Diablada pisco

Shake with ice.

Strain into a chilled champagne flute.

Top with 90 ml rosé champagne.

Adapted from a recipe by Meaghan Dorman, Raines Law Room, New York City.
