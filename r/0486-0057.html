Kentucky Flyer

Shake with ice:

60 ml rye whiskey

22.5 ml Luxardo maraschino liqueur

15 ml fresh lemon juice

1.5 fresh mint leaves

Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Garnish with 5 brandied cherries.

Adapted from a recipe by Sierra Zimei, Four Seasons, San Francisco.

“This is a play off of the Aviation but with good ol’ ’merican ingredients.”—Sierra Zimei
