Thai Cobbler

3 basil leaves (preferably Thai basil)

2 lemon wedges

30 ml fresh peach puree

15 ml Domaine de Canton ginger liqueur

60 ml Maker’s Mark bourbon

1 dash Angostura bitters

1 basil leaf, as garnish

1 peach slice or lemon twist, as garnish

Gently muddle basil and lemon in a mixing glass. Add ice and the remaining ingredients. Shake and strain into an old-fashioned glass with fresh ice. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Benjamin Tubbs, Brian’s Pourhouse, Hood River, OR.

“As some may know, Ty Cobb’s nickname was the “Georgia Peach.” Well, with the addition of some “Thai” flavors and a play on words as well as on the Whiskey Smash, this is what I’ve come up with. Please enjoy!”—Benjamin Tubbs
