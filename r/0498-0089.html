Peach Blossom

60 ml Dutch’s Spirits peach brandy

15 ml fresh orange juice

7.5 ml Monin vanilla syrup

2 to 3 drops orange blossom water

Shake over ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Adapted from a recipe by Jake Sher, Mix @ The Crowne Plaza Hotel, White Plains, NY, and Jonathan Forester, Dutch’s Spirits, NY.

gaz sez: Run, don’t walk, to get the Dutch Spirits peach brandy. It’ not a liqueur like other “peach brandies” on the market. This is the stuff that Americans were making back in the 1700s. Real brandy, made from real peaches. The Peach Blossom cocktail is a good way to show off this spirit, with the other ingredients adding nuances, but coming nowhere close to shadowing the base spirit.
