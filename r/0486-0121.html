Virgin de Guadalupe

45 ml blanco tequila

15 ml yellow Chartreuse

15 ml fresh lime juice

30 ml fresh grapefruit juice

9 cl ginger beer (preferably spicy and slightly sweet)

1 long grapefruit twist, as garnish

Build in highball glass. Top with ginger beer. Squeeze the twist over the drink, then add as garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Adrian Biggs, La Descarga, Los Angeles.

“Refreshing, light, and delicious. I love the Paloma cocktail, which was my inspiration for creating this libation. Cheers!”—Adrian Biggs

gaz sez: The Chartreuse brings a complexity to this baby, and she plays well with the tequila, too. Adrian didn’t push the envelope to come up with this one, but he sure as hell nailed the ingredients and the proportions.
