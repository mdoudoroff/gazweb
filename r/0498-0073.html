Extra Virgin Olive Oil-Infused Lagavulin

Combine:

15 cl extra virgin olive oil

40 cl Lagavulin 12 Year Old

Let sit for 4 hours, stirring occasionally. Freeze for 12 hours. Skim the oil from the top, fine-strain, and bottle.

Adapted from a recipe by George Bagos, A for Athens Hotel, Athens, Greece.
