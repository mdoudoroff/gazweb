Jive Turkey

Stir with ice:

30 ml Wild Turkey rye whiskey

22.5 ml Buffalo Trace bourbon

22.5 ml Amaro CioCiaro

22.5 ml dry vermouth

7.5 ml St. Germain

1 dash Angostura bitters

Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Adapted from a recipe by Jessica Gonzalez, Death & Co., New York City.

“I love to blend bourbon and rye. This is inspired by the Brooklyn Cocktail as well.”—Jessica Gonzalez

gaz sez: Jessica knows where my tastes lie, and the Jive Turkey lives right on my street, just down the road apiece from my Manhattan. The St. Germain in this one adds a very interesting touch, and it’s because the recipe calls for such a small amount of it that this drink is so well balanced.
