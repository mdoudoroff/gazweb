Marmalade Sour

Combine in a mixing glass:

1 egg white

2 barspoons orange marmalade

60 ml Corralejo blanco tequila

30 ml fresh lemon juice

3 dashes Scrappy’s Lavender bitters

Dry shake.

Shake with ice.

Double-strain into a chilled coupe.

Garnish with a flamed orange twist.

Adapted from a recipe by Casey Robison, Barrio, Seattle.

gaz sez: If you haven’t tried Scrappy’s Lavender bitters (www.scrappysbitters.com), then this is a drink that will serve you well if you make it in order to see what they can bring to a cocktail. The interplay between the marmalade and the bitters is just incredible in the Marmalade Sour, and the tequila serves as a gracious host for the match.
