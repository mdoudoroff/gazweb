101 Best New Cocktails

Robert Gonzalez

50 ml Sheep Dip Blended Malt Scotch Whisky

10 ml Dolin sweet vermouth

10 ml Punt E Mes

10 ml agave syrup

2 dashes Regans’ Orange Bitters No. 6

1 bourbon soaked cherry, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled coupette. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Aaron Shuttleworth, The White Hart, Sydney, Australia.

“This cocktail remained unnamed until I served it to a customer one day, who decided that Robert Gonzalez was fitting for what is essentially a Rob Roy with the addition of Agave. The drink was made originally for a gentleman who wanted a softer scotch-based drink. It’s become a nice little bridging drink for people who wouldn’t normally find a classic scotch cocktail to their liking. The Sheep Dip is a very smooth blend of single malts and mixes well with the spicy punch of the Punt E Mes and drier sweetness of the Dolin. The addition of agave gives the drink a nice rich roundness.” Aaron Shuttleworth.
