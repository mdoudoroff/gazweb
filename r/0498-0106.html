101 Best New Cocktails

Seasons Change

60 ml Michter’s US-1 Single Barrel Straight Rye

3 dashes Peychaud’s bitters

3 dashes Roast Chestnut Tincture

1 barspoon maple syrup

1 orange twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled rocks glass. Flame the twist over the drink, then add as garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Grant Dingwall, Andiamo, Auckland, New Zealand.
