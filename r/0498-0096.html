Butternut Squash Simple Syrup

Combine in a saucepan:

400 g (2 cups) brown sugar

720 ml water

2 cups of cubed butternut squash

Bring to boil. Stir until the sugar dissolves, and then reduce the heat and simmer for 45 minutes. Let the syrup cool to infuse further. Strain thoroughly and store in the refrigerator.
