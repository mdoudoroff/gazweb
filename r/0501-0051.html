Eric Grenier’s Cinnamon Vanilla Syrup

Combine in a non-reactive saucepan over a medium heat:

1 cup water

3 cinnamon sticks

1 cup demerara sugar

Stir until the sugar is dissolved. Split and scrape one vanilla bean and steep both the bean and seeds in the mixture for 10 minutes. Allow to cool, strain out the solids and bottle. Add a splash of Smith & Cross Navy Strength Rum to bottle to prolong the useful life of the syrup.

Adapted from a recipe by Eric Grenier, Honor Kitchen & Cocktails, Emeryville , CA
