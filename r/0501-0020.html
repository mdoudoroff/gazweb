Bristol Old Fashioned N°2

15 ml Maxime Hoerth’s Hot Wine Shrub

3 dashes Bittermens Burlesque Bitters

50 ml Gosling’s Black Seal rum

1 cinnamon stick, as garnish

1 star anise, as garnish

1 orange twist, as garnish

Pour the shrub and the bitters into a chilled old-fashioned glass. Add half of the rum and a few ice cubes and stir. Add more ice and the second half of the rum. Add the garnishes.

P.S.: Try the shrub in your coffee of a morning. It’s pretty deeeelish!

“This cocktail reminds me of my childhood. Born in Alsace (north-east of France) I grew up with the X-mas flavours and I wanted to join those flavours to my favorite cocktail that is the old-fashioned (made with rum, gin, cognac, bourbon… ). This is how and why that cocktail was created. X-mas flavours in a gentleman cold drink.”—Maxime Hoerth.
