101 Best New Cocktails

Atom Limo

Build in an iced rocks glass:

35 ml Wild Turkey Bourbon (101 Proof)

35 ml Lustau Pedro Ximenez sherry

15 ml fresh lemon juice

40 ml fresh apple juice

2 pieces ginger

Fill with ginger ale.

Garnish with 1 mint sprig.

Garnish with 1 apple slice.

Garnish with 1 fresh cherry.

Adapted from a recipe by Stephan Hinz, Shepheard American Bar, Cologne, Germany.

“Cheers!”—Stephan Hinz. Stephan suggests serving this drink in a ball jar.
