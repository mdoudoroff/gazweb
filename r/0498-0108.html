101 Best New Cocktails

Skid Rosa

1 medium strawberry

3 cucumber slices

30 ml Il Conte Stella Rosa “Stella Rosso” semi-sweet wine

15 ml Ketel One Citroen

45 ml fresh sour mix

1 cucumber wheel, as garnish

Muddle the strawberry and cucumber. Add ice and the remaining ingredients. Shake and fine-strain into an ice-filled collins glass. Add the garnish. Enjoy.

Adapted from a recipe by Eric Tecosky, Jones Hollywood, Los Angeles.
