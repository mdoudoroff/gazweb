101 Best New Cocktails

Reunion Island

60 ml Bols Genever

22.5 ml Coffee-Infused Gran Classico

15 ml Carpano Antica Formula

1 orange twist, as garnish

Stir all ingredients with ice mindfully. Pour into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass. Squeeze the garnish over the drink first, then sent afloat on top.

Adapted from a recipe by Laura Lindsay, Providence Restaurant, Los Angeles, CA.

“This cocktail takes its name from the ‘Noble Tree’ that Dutch explorers took from Java, whose offshoots were planted on Reunion Island, east of Madagascar. This tribute of Bols Genever, coffee-infused Gran Classico, and Carpano Antica pays homage to the role the Dutch played in the spread of coffee and spices around the world.” Laura Lindsay.
