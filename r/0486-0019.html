Almond Cardamom Sugar

Combine in a food processor:

250 g sweet almonds (shelled)

125 g superfine sugar

18 oz simple syrup

4 oz water

4 cardamom pods

grated nutmeg (Â¼ of a nutmeg)

Filter only 1 time to keep some of the texture. Store in fridge.
