Here’s Johnnie

50 ml Johnnie Walker Gold

30 ml The Bitter Truth Apricot Liqueur

25 ml fresh lemon juice

2 thin slices fresh ginger

2 dashes The Bitter Truth Orange bitters

1 orange slice, as garnish

Combine all the ingredients in shaker, muddling the ginger to open up the flavour. Add ice and shake hard for about 10 seconds, then double-strain into a chilled coupe. Add the garnish to the edge of the glass.

Adapted from a recipe by Bas Verhoeven, Cocktailbar Mundial, Eindhoven, Netherlands.

“A cocktail with an amazing taste that ticks all the boxes. Created with the philosophy less is more, the few ingredients combine amazingly well. This has made it a big favorite amongst our guests and even has the guests who usually avoid whiskey drinks asking for it.” Bas Verhoeven.
