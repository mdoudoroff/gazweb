Free Rider

30 ml Lemon Hart 151 Demerara rum

30 ml Bénédictine

15 ml Fernet Branca

45 ml pineapple juice

22.5 ml lime juice

7.5 ml demerara syrup

2 dashes Angostura bitters

1 mint leaf, as garnish

Shake and strain into a Tiki mug. Fill with crushed ice and add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Billy Helmkamp, The Whistler, Chicago, IL.

“I created this drink for February’s Mixology Monday, the theme of which was Tiki. Much like the song it was named after, this drink is a mash-up of sorts. It was inspired by two of my favorite cocktails at the moment: Robert Hess’ Voyager and Jeffrey Morganthaler’s Kingston Club.”—Billy Helmkamp
