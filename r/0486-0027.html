The Double “D”

60 ml Double Cross vodka

7.5 ml Pear Elixir

1 dash Dale DeGroff’s Pimento bitters

Miniature bottled pear, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a cocktail glass. Place the pear garnish on a cocktail pick in the bottom of the glass.

Miniature bottled pears are available through Double Cross Vodka (www.doublecrossvodka.com) for free.

Adapted from a recipe by Dale DeGroff, New York City.

This autumnal martini-style cocktail brings Double Cross vodka and Dale DeGroff together in its name.
