Skott Reader’s Ginger Tea

Thinly slice 480 grams (1 pound) of fresh ginger. Put in a medium saucepan and add water to cover. Bring to a rolling boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 2 to 3 hours. Let cool, strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a mason jar, and store in the refrigerator.

Adapted from a recipe by Skott Reader, The Royal Cuckoo, San Francisco, CA.
