Sushi Companion

2 cilantro sprigs

4 cucumber chunks (about 1 inch)

15 ml fresh lime juice

15 ml sugar syrup (1:1)

45 ml Hendrick’s gin

15 ml Junmai sake (dry)

1 barspoon green Chartreuse

1 lime wheel, as garnish

1 bruised cilantro sprig, as garnish

Muddle the cilantro, cucumber, lime juice, and syrup in mixing glass. Add ice and the remaining ingredients. Shake it hard to wake it up and strain into a double rocks glass full of fresh ice. Add the garnishes.

Adapted from a recipe by Dimitar Dimitrov, Planet Club, Sofia, Bulgaria.

“I’ve first created this drink with Wokka Sake Vodka in 2007 for Oishii Boston, when I was working in US after the Cocktails in the Country training program and was selling very good but made with gin and sake has more complexity.” Dimitar Dimitrov.

gaz sez: Dimitar makes fabulous use of the Hendrick’s gin in this recipe, and I love the fact that he kept going back to his original formula and tinkering with it until he was happy with the results. And I also love what the Chartreuse does to this drink. It’s a very small amount, but it really makes its presence known.
