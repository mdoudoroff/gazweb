Ran Duan’s Homemade Spice Syrup

2 cinnamon sticks

2 star anise

9 cloves

4 cardamom pods

2 liters water

Combine in a large saucepan. Bring to boil, reduce the heat, and simmer for 15 minutes until you get a nice light golden color from spice.

Add:

1.2 kg (16 oz) sugar

Stir and simmer for 5 more minutes. Cool and transfer to bottles. This should make enough for about two 750-ml bottles.

Adapted from a recipe by Ran Duan, Sichuan Garden 2, Boston.
