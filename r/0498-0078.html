Motorcar Cocktail

45 ml Dewar’s 12 Year Old

30 ml Dolin Rouge vermouth

10 ml Campari

1 barspoon sugar syrup

1 dash Fee Brothers chocolate bitters

1 orange twist, as garnish

Stir over large ice cubes and strain into a chilled fancy cocktail glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Lou Dare, The Alchemist, Melbourne, Australia.

“Inspired by a cocktail shown to me by one of the guys from Milk & Honey, called a Right Hand, which essentially uses rum in place of the scotch. However, I tinkered with the proportions and named it the Motorcar as a tip of the hat to John Dewar, the founder of Dewar’s scotch and apparently the first man in the UK to own a Motorcar.” Lou Dare.

gaz sez: I’d never have dreamed of pairing Campari with scotch, but it works very well indeed in this cocktail. The chocolate bitters provide an interesting aspect, too, even though they play a very small role in this drink.
