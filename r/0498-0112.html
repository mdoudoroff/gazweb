Sorriso

30 ml Plymouth gin

30 ml Absolut Pears

15 ml Luxardo maraschino liqueur

15 ml cream sherry

2 dashes Angostura bitters

1 lemon twist, as garnish

1 orange twist, as garnish

1 Luxardo marasca cherry, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Squeeze the twists over the drink, then add as garnishes with the cherry.

Adapted from a recipe by Francesco Lafranconi, Southern Wine and Spirits of America, Las Vegas, NV.

“Sorriso means ‘smile’ in Italian, I believe that every drink should be always served with a smile:) Salute!” Francesco Lafranconi.
