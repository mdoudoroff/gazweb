101 Best New Cocktails

10 Scotch Cigars

45 ml Laphroaig 10 Year Old scotch whisky

22.5 ml Fernet Branca

15 ml rich demerara simple syrup

1 barspoon verjus blanc

1 orange twist

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled glass. Flame the orange twist over the drink, then discard.

Adapted from a recipe by Noah Heaney, Argyll Gastropub, Denver.

“The amount of water introduced by the stir is crucial. Using Kold-Draft I never stir more than 30 seconds. No one likes a diluted cocktail…”—Noah Heaney.
