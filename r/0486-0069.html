Napoleon’s Itch

30 ml Mandarine Napoléon

22.5 ml Leopold’s Small Batch gin

22.5 ml Dolin Blanc vermouth

22.5 ml fresh lemon juice

3 to 4 drops Regans’ Orange Bitters No. 6

1 Luxardo maraschino cherry, as garnish

Shake over ice and double-strain into chilled small cocktail glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Toby McMillen, Mistral Restaurant & Bar, Redwood City, CA.

“I based this upon the classic Corpse Reviver #2.” Toby McMillen.
