Tap Out

5 ml Bénédictine

3 dashes Bittermens Xocolatl Mole bitters

50 ml Tapatio Añejo

2 dashes Chartreuse Elixir

1 small raw sugar cube

1 orange twist, as garnish

Rinse a chilled rocks glass with the Bénédictine and bitters. Stir the remaining ingredients over ice until the sugar is dissolved and strain into the glass. Flame the twist over the drink, then add as garnish. Imbibe until submission is reached!

Adapted from a recipe by Chris Lacey, Bikeshed Theatre Bar, Exeter, UK.

“The thought behind this comes from my love of Sazeracs and tequila so I needed some way of combining the two. The vegetal notes in the Tapatio añejo are enhanced with the elixir vegetal and enriched with the Bénédictine and Xocolatl bitters. Like the Sazerac, it’s a drink that keeps giving after the last sip. Hope you enjoy it and remember Make Drinks not War!” Chris Lacey.
