Camouflage

40 ml gin

30 ml Cocchi Americano

40 ml carrot juice

15 ml fresh lime juice

15 ml sandalwood syrup

40 ml kombucha tea

1 dash grapefruit bitters

1 dash Peychaud’s bitters

Shake all the ingredients apart from the kombucha tea over ice, then fine-strain into a metal pineapple. Top with the kombucha tea. Garnish with a piece of sandalwood incense stood up on an orange wheel—place the incense stick in the vessel and light it. Put the top on the pineapple and serve the drink.

Simone Caporale, Artesian Bar, Langham Hotel, London, UK

“Nothing is what it seems! Pineapple, the symbol of hospitality is hiding the most unexpected flavor combinations!”—Simone Caporale
