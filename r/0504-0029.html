Ginger in Pearadise

60 ml Tequila Avion Reposado

37.5 ml Santa Cruz organic pear nectar (or other high quality pear nectar)

22.5 ml fresh ginger juice (juiced through juicer, or blended and strained)

30 ml agave nectar

30 ml fresh squeezed lime juice

A few small pieces of candied ginger, as garnish

Shake over ice and strain into an ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Add the garnish.

David Heid, City Cellar Wine Bar and Grill, West Palm Beach, Florida, USA

“This drink is very refreshing, and has just the right amount of kick with the fresh ginger,”—David Hein
