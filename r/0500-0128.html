Homemade Tonic Syrup

Combine in a sauce pan:

4 cups water

3 cups superfine sugar

Heat and stir until the sugar dissolves.

Add:

3 tablespoons chinchona bark powder

zest of 3 limes

juice of 3 limes

3 lemongrass stalks, chopped

Simmer 25 minutes. The chinchona bark powder should be dissolved.

Remove from heat and cool.

Filter through cheesecloth and bottle.

Store in the refrigerator.

Adapted from a recipe by Kevin Ludwig that appears on the Imbibe Magazine web site (http://www.imbibemagazine.com/Homemade-Tonic-Water-Recipe).
