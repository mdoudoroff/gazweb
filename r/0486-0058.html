Laissez Les Bons Temps Rouler!

Prepare a chilled cocktail glass rinsed with absinthe.

Stir with ice:

60 ml Russell’s Reserve 6-year-old rye whiskey

15 ml Cherry Heering

15 ml Domain de Canton ginger liqueur

2 dashes Regans’ Orange Bitters No. 6

Strain into the prepared glass.

Garnish with an orange twist.

Adapted from a recipe by Bob Brunner, Paragon Restaurant & Bar, Portland, OR.

“Let the good times roll! This cocktail was featured in the May 2010 issue of Market Watch magazine. A modern variation on the classic Sazerac cocktail, this one captivates!”—Bob Brunner
