Tomatini

1 whole ripe tomato

10 ml Basalico white balsamic vinegar

5 ml sugar syrup

1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

1 pinch smoked sea salt

40 ml Ketel One vodka

Freshly ground pepper, as garnish

1 cherry tomato, as garnish

Muddle the tomato, vinegar, and sugar syrup in a mixing glass with the salt and pepper. Add ice and the vodka. Shake hard and double-strain into chilled cocktail glass. Add the garnishes.

Adapted from a recipe by Sany Bacsi, La Petite Maison, Dubai.

“More hard you shake it more foam you going to get which stay longer. To make the garnish a bit more fancy leave a small piece of green stem and cut the cherry tomato on the side and dunk it a bit of salt and pepper mix. Hi Gaz!
