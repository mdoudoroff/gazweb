Smokin’ Orange

1 lemon twist, as garnish

9 cl Combier liqueur d’orange

15 ml Laphroaig 10 Year Old scotch whisky

15 ml fresh lemon juice

Rub the yellow part of the lemon twist on the lip of a chilled whisky tumbler and drop it in; add ice. Set aside. Shake all ingredients over ice for about 20 and strain into the glass.

Adapted from a recipe by Tarcisio Costa, Alfama, New York City.

“For lovers of smoky flavors, this is a great fall/winter cocktail.”—Tarcisio Costa
