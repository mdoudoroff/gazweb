101 Best New Cocktails

Midori Sour

45 ml Beefeater gin

15 ml Midori

22.5 ml fresh honeydew melon juice

22.5 ml fresh lemon juice

15 ml simple syrup

1 egg white

3 dashes of verjus blanc

pinch of citrus salt and black pepper

Shake violently and serve up in a frozen Coupe.

Garnish with 3 melon balls on a skewer sprinkled with cracked pepper.

Naren Young, www.forkandshaker.com, New York, USA
