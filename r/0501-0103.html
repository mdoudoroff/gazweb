The Rusty Apple

30 ml Chivas Regal

45 ml Berentzen Apfel Korn

30 ml Honeycrisp apple juice

15 ml fresh lemon juice

15 ml agave nectar

Drambuie Foam

1 apple fan, as garnish

freshly grated cinnamon, as garnish

Dispense enough Drambuie foam to fill a chilled coupe glass ⅓ of the way. Shake the rest of the ingredients over ice and strain into the chilled coupe through one part of the foam to avoid ruining the separation effect. Finish off with an apple fan and some freshly grated cinnamon on the surface of the foam.

Adapted from a recipe by Christopher James, The Ryland Inn, Whitehouse Station, NJ.

“This cocktail is intended to be a twisted, seasonal mash-up of an Apple Martini and a Rusty Nail,”—Christopher James.
