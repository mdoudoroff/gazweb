101 Best New Cocktails

Black Gold

30 ml Michter’s Rye Whiskey

15 ml Bärenjäger Honey Liqueur

2 bar spoons Suze Saveur d’autrefois

3 dashes Fee Brothers Whiskey Barrel-Aged Bitters

1 Filthy Food Black Cherry, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Add the garnish.

Megan Bardoe, Lulu in the Grove, Miami, FL
