Gunpowder Green Tea Simple Syrup

Combine in a pint glass:

8 oz demerara sugar

3 gunpowder green tea bags

Fill with boiling water.

Stir to dissolve.

Steep 3-5 minutes.

Remove tea bags, cool and refrigerate.

Adapted from a recipe by Anthony DeSerio, Coastal Gourmet Group. Aspen Restaurant/Latitude 41 Shipyard Tavern, Mystic, CT.
