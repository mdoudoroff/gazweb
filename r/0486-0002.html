A Very Cooperative Cocktail

Stir with ice:

30 ml aged rum

30 ml American Fruits Sour Cherry Cordial or Cherry Heering

30 ml cold brewed chai tea

15 ml simple syrup (1:1)

Strain into a chilled champagne coupe.

Garnish with a lemon twist.

Recipe by gaz regan.

Created for the Cornwall Community Co-Op, Cornwall-on-Hudson, New York, for a benefit featuring John Charles Thomas on trumpet and Bari Mort at the piano, July, 2010.
