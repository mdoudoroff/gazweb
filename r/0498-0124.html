Licorice Honey Syrup

First, make a decoction of the licorice: measure out .5 cup of dried licorice root into a saucepan. Add 500 ml (2.25 cups) water. Bring the water to a boil, add 500 g (2.5 cups) sugar, and stir.

Add .5 cup honey while the “tea” is still warm. Stir until the honey dissolves fully and let it simmer for about 20 minutes. Strain, cool and then pour into a glass jar with a tight lid. Store your syrup in the refrigerator.

Adapted from a recipe by Humberto Marques, 1105 cocktail bar, Copenhagen.
