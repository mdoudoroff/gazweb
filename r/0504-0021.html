101 Best New Cocktails

Cold Stare

45 ml Pierde Almas La Puritita Verdá

30 ml cold pressed coffee

15 ml Luxardo Amaro Abano

7.5 ml St. George Raspberry Liqueur

1 dash Fee Brothers Black Walnut Bitters

Stir over ice for only about 15 turns (should just be slightly diluted and not fully chilled), and strain into a footed rocks glass or coupe. No garnish.

Peter Vestinos, The Winchester/The BarMedic, Chicago, IL USA.

Peter says: “Flavor Profile- Rich, Herbal, Coffee, Smoke, Lush mouth feel from Amaro and Liqueur; finishes bitter-sweet.

“The smoke of the mescal and the floral dark coffee flavors blend beautifully together. The rich flavor of the St. G raspberry liqueur runs beneath all the other elements, typing them to gather and giving a bit of a fresh note to what otherwise could be a heavy cocktail. And the amaro acts as a ‘brace’ to hold the cocktail upright and finish it off with a slight bitter note. Good for brown spirit drinker.”—Peter Vestinos

gaz sez: I used Fee Brothers’ Black Walnut bitters when testing this, and they brought an interesting dimension to the drink.
