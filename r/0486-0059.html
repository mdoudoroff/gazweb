Lock and Key

Shake with ice:

45 ml Buffalo Trace bourbon

15 ml Strega

15 ml Carpano Antica

15 ml fresh lemon juice

Double-strain into a chilled coupe.

Garnish with 3 drops Fee Brothers Whiskey Barrel Aged bitters.

Garnish with a mint leaf.

Adapted from a recipe by Summer Voelker, Salt of the Earth, Pittsburgh.

“We could not get Strega in for a while and substituted .5 oz yellow Chartreuse. Still delicious!”—Summer Voelker

gaz sez: I love the way in which the Buffalo Trace retains its integrity in this drink. It takes on the Strega and the Carpano, giving them room to dance in the ring, but it never lets either one of them get their licks in. Nice work if you can get it.
