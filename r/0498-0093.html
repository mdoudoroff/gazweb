101 Best New Cocktails

Pussycat

60 ml rye whiskey (Willett, Sazerac, or Michter’s)

30 ml Skott Reader’s Ginger Tea

15 ml fresh lemon juice

15 ml Aperol

1 dash simple syrup

1 orange twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Skott Reader, The Royal Cuckoo, San Francisco, CA.
