The Haji Sling

Shake with ice:

20 ml Angostura Bitters

20 ml Cherry Heering

20 ml Bénédictine

20 ml fresh lime juice

12.5 ml orgeat

Strain into an ice-filled highball glass.

Float ½ oz brown rum, to taste.

Garnish with 1 lime wheel and 1 cherry.

Ian Loh, Helvetica, Perth, Australia

“For the love of Angostura Bitters. A twist on the Singapore Sling, named after the quirky Haji Laneway in Singapore,”—Ian Loh
