101 Best New Cocktails

Piano

1.75 oz Castello Banfi Grappa Di Brunello

.25 oz Meletti Sambuca

1 oz fresh lemon juice

.5 oz Hibiscus Syrup

1 egg white

Add all of the ingredients to a mixing glass and vigorously dry shake for about 20 seconds. Add ice and shake for an additional 10 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe.

Garnish with a lemon twist (discard the zest after expressing the oils over the drink).

Garnish with 5 drops Angostura bitters.

Sam Nelis, Pizzeria Verita, Burlington, Vermont, USA
