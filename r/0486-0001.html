After-Dinner Sazerac

Prepare a chilled old-fashioned glass rinsed with Galliano Ristretto.

Combine in a mixing glass:

1 dash Angostura bitters

1 sugar cube

Stir to dissolve.

Add:

60 ml Yamazaki Single Malt 12-Year-Old whisky

Stir with ice.

Strain into the prepared glass.

Garnish with 1 kaffir lime leaf (see below)

For the kaffir lime leaf, break or “crack” the leaf in four different places lengthwise over the drink to get the aromas out, then run it over the rim of the glass and discard.

Adapted from a recipe by Giancarlo Quiroz Jesus, Corner Bar, Auckland, New Zealand.

“This cocktail was a semi-finalist for the New Zealand Suntory Cup Cocktail Competition 2010, chosen out of 100. Easy to drink and make, definitely the cocktail after a well deserved dinner.”—Giancarlo Quiroz Jesus.
