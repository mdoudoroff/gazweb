Saloon Keeper’s Daughter

60 ml Michter’s US-1 Single Barrel Straight Rye

15 ml Luxardo maraschino liqueur

15 ml fresh lemon juice

7.5 ml house-made grenadine

1 dash orange bitters

1 lemon twist

Shake over cracked ice and strain into chilled cocktail glass. Squeeze the twist over drink and run around the rim of the glass, then discard.

Adapted from a recipe by Katie Loeb, Oyster House, Philadelphia, PA.

“This is one of the very first cocktails I ever created. I was going for that Old School pre-Prohibition style of drink with enough sweetness to make it a Girly Whiskey drink that men wouldn’t be embarrassed to order. It’s sort of a tough girl’s Cosmo!” Katie Loeb.
