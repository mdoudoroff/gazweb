Rising Sun

52.5 ml Yamazaki 12 Year whisky

15 ml yuzu juice

7.5 ml ginger syrup

1 barspoon honey syrup

30 ml Chikurin Hou Hou Shu “Bubble Bubble Sake”

1 fig slice skewered with long picks in the form of an X, as garnish

Combine all ingredients except sake in a cocktail shaker with ice. Hard-shake and double-strain into a chilled highball glass (no ice) or cocktail glass. Add the sake and swirl gently. If serving in a cocktail glass, add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Steve Garcia, Broadway by Amar Santana, Laguna Beach, CA.

“This cocktail is based upon a classic fizz, hence the highball glass, NO ICE. The effervescence comes from the sparkling sake instead of soda. For some clients, a cocktail glass might make more sense. When I created this drink, it just so happened that figs were in season, so I sliced one and speared it with 2 bamboo swords. The effect was dual: the round dark red fruit appeared to rise from the cocktail, and it also looked like a coat of arms. Also, it is important to note that I use Chikurin Hou Hou Shu in the blue bottle, not the Hana in the pink.” Steve Garcia.
