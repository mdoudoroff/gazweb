Outono (Autumn, in Portuguese)

45 ml Busnel Calvados VSOP

60 ml peach nectar

15 ml Galliano L’Autentico

15 ml fresh lemon juice

1-2 dashes Angostura bitters

1 apple slice (red or green, depending on your preference), as garnish

Shake over ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Add the garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Tarcisio Costa, Alfama Restaurant, New York City.

“As obvious as the name may sound, it really does taste just like Autumn. And while listening to Ella Fitzgerald’s song ‘Autumn in New York’ with its refrain ‘Autumn in New York, why does it seem so inviting?’ I came up with this drink. Cheers!” Tarcisio Costa.
