101 Best New Cocktails

Mute Appeal

45 ml Laird’s Seven-and-a-half Year Old Apple Brandy

22.5 ml Domaine de Canton ginger liqueur

15 ml Averna

15 ml Pimm’s No. 1 Cup

1 dash Regans’ Orange Bitters No. 6

1 orange twist, as garnish

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Flame the twist over the drink, then add as garnish.

Adapted from a recipe by Phoebe Esmon, The Farmers Cabinet, Philadelphia.

“Autumn wins you best by this  its mute appeal to sympathy for its decay.”  Robert Browning, Paracelsus
