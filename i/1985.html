Bols Creme de Cacao (white)

Bols Creme de Cacao (white) is a proprietary example of crème de cacao (white).

White crème de cacao is a generic for a colorless liqueur with chocolate flavoring in a neutral spirits base. Virtually all liqueur companies with broad product ranges produce a crème de cacao, usually in both white (clear) & brown-colored versions. Other than the coloration, the products are usually identical.
