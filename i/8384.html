Darroze 10 Year

Darroze 10 Year is a proprietary example of armagnac.

Armagnac is generic for the aged grape brandies from the Armagnac region of France with distinct distillation procedures which also define it from Cognac or other brandies. Used uncommonly in cocktails or other mixed drinks, but notably in the Rose Marie Cocktail.
