gunpowder green tea leaves

Also known as gunpowder tea leaves, gunpowder green tea bags

Gunpowder green tea is a style of green tea produce in Zhejiang province, China. It typically features tiny tightly-rolled shot-like balls of leaves (“pearls”) with a vaguely gray/metallic cast. The flavor is usually slightly smokey in addition to the expected vegetal characteristics.
