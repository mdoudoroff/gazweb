Carpano Punt e Mes

Proprietary red vermouth with extra bitter/botanical flavoring produced by the house of Carpano (kar-PAH-no). Simultaneously one of the sweetest and bitterest vermouths. Arguably, Punt e Mes qualifies as a bottled, low-proof aperitif cocktail.

Vermouth con bitter is generic for a venerable Turinese style of vermouth with extra botanical flavoring to enhance its bitter, herbal character, and also (typically) further sweetened for balance.
