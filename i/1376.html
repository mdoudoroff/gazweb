Hayman’s Old Tom Gin

Also known as Hayman’s

Proprietary old tom gin produced in England.

Old Tom gin is a generic category for slightly sweetened, richly-flavored gin of a very early variety; the “missing link” between malty Dutch genever (“Holland” gin) and the London Dry Gin style.

OriginEngland
ABV40.0%
