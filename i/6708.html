Averna Limon de Sicilia

Averna Limon de Sicilia is a proprietary example of limoncello.

Limoncello is generic for a sugar-sweetened Italian spirit, native to the Naples vicinity, popularly of both home made and commercially bottled varieties, with an intense lemon flavor deriving from lemon peels soaked a month or more in grain neutral spirits.
