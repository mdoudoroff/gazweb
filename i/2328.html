George Dickel No. 12

George Dickel No. 12 is a proprietary example of tennessee whiskey.

Tennessee whiskey is a generic category for U.S. single-malt whiskey distilled from predominately corn mash with wheat or rye and filtered through charcoal. The charcoal filtering is principally what distinguishes Tennessee whiskey from bourbon. Jack Daniels and George Dickel are currently produced brands of Tennessee whiskey.
