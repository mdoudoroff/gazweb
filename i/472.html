virgin islands rum

Also known as st. croix rum

Virgin Islands rum is generic for the rums of Virgin Islands, most of which comes from St. Croix. Contemporary rum from St. Croix is all of light-bodied character. Cruzan (CREWZ-shun) is a noted producer. Unless otherwise specified, a colorless or pale spirit is implied.
