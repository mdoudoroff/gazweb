sour mix

Also known as sweet & sour mix, lemon sour mix, lemon mix, fresh sour mix

Sour mix is a generic category for mixture a of sugar, water and natural lemon and/or lime juices and/or artificial flavoring, glycerin, preservatives. Commercial sour mixes should generally be avoided.

To make a high quality homemade sour mix: combine two parts fresh lemon juice to one part sugar syrup.
