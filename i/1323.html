Laird’s 100-Proof Bonded Apple Brandy

Also known as Laird’s Bonded Apple Brandy, Laird’s Bonded Applejack

Proprietary straight American apple brandy (applejack). Outstanding in mixed drinks.

American apple brandy is generic for brandy distilled from apples in the USA. Often another term for applejack. Marketed as a straight distillate with only water dilution to regulate the proof, or, less frequently, blended with neutral spirits. Excellent in cocktails.

The de facto benchmark is Laird’s Bonded Apple Brandy.
