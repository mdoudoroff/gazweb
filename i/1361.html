Wray & Nephew White Overproof Rum

Proprietary clear light rum distilled from molasses and bottled at 62.8% alc/vol. Of limited use in mixed drinks, but the Jamaican product carries more flavor than the equivalents from Puerto Rico or Santa Cruz.

Overproof Jamaican white rum is generic for the traditional, unaged (straight-from-the-still) molasses-based rums of Jamaica popular in the local market. The best-known contemporary example is Wray & Nephew White Overproof Rum.
