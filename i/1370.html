The Bitter Truth Celery Bitters

Proprietary German revival of celery bitters.

Celery bitters is a 19th century-style bitters featuring celery seed flavors called for in cocktail recipe guides of the period. A noted brand was Gold Lion Celery Bitters, produced by Cook & Bernheimer, famed whiskey distillers of yesteryear. At least one company made a combination of pepsin/celery bitters.
