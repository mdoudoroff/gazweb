Don Q Cristal

Don Q Cristal is a proprietary example of white puerto rican rum.

White Puerto Rican rum is generic for the light-bodied aged rums of Puerto Rico that have been filtered of their color (and some of their flavor) to produce a largely-colorless spirit for use in mixed drinks.
