Filthy Food Black Cherry

Also known as Filthy Black Cherry

Proprietary brandied amarena cherries intended for use as drink garnishes.

Brandied cherry refers to a commercial or home-made cherry preserved in sugar syrup and brandy, used as garnish or flavoring additive uncommonly in individual mixed drinks and punches.
