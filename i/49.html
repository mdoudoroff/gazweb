Pimm’s Cup #1

Proprietary British bottled spiced gin sling cocktail to which is added a mixer, like soda water, ginger ale, ginger beer, or 7-Up in a tall glass with ice.

Fruit cup is generic for a concentrate of gin (or other spirit), botanicals and fruit essences and concentrates intended to be diluted with a soft drink prior to serving. An important British bottled cocktail tradition for which Pimm’s No. 1 is probably the defining example.
