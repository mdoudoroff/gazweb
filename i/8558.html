Bols Corenwyn 6 Year

Bols Corenwyn 6 Year is a proprietary example of korenwijn.

Korenwijn is a relatively recently-introduced Dutch style of genever including at least 51% malt wine in the blend, containing no more than 20 grams sugar/liter and bottled at 35% ABV or more. The result is a comparatively malt-forward, “sweet tasting” smooth liquor as one might sip neat or on the rocks. Korenwijn may be bottled unaged or aged at least one year in barrels of less than 700 liters.

ABV40.0%
SubstituteNo real substitute, although some recipes that call for genever can produce agreeable results when other spirits are substituted. An unpeated Irish whiskey can sometimes work as a substitute when an oude genever is called for.
