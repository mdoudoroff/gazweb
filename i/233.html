coffee-flavored liqueur

Also known as creme de kaffee

Coffee-flavored liqueur is a generic category for liqueur containing natural and/or artificial coffee flavoring in neutral spirit or rum base and sweetener. Examples are Kahlua, Tia Maria, and creme de café, and creme de moka.
