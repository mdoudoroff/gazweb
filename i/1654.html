rooibus (brewed)

Also known as rooibus tea, rooibos tea, redbush tea

Rooibus is generic for a hot infusion of the dried needles of a medicinal herb plant of the same name, native to South Africa. Infusions of rooibus inherit its reddish brown hue and rich, nutty, almost malty flavor. A “green” rooibus (unoxidized) is also produced, but this style is relatively rare and expensive.

Note: rooibus is sometimes sold as "red tea". This nomenclature is problematic, because rooibus is not tea, and the fully-oxidized tea that Westerners commonly call “black tea” is actually called “red tea” in China.

OriginSouth Africa
