sencha

Sencha is the preeminent tea of Japan, produced by steaming the leaves to arrest oxidation and then dried. Flavor is intensely vegetal. The (tannic) bitterness of sencha is strongly related to the temperature of the water in which it is brewed. Cold brewing sencha or brewing at 175ยบ-185ยบ F will yeild a minimally bitter brew.
