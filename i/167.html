Mandarine Napoleon

Proprietary Belgian liqueur with mandarin orange flavoring and a Cognac base. The term mandarin & tangerine are truly interchangeable, but commercially "tangerine" refers to the variety with a red-orange skin. The flavor is quite similar, regardless.

Mandarin liqueur is a generic category for mandarin orange liqueur in either neutral spirit or brandy base. The term mandarin and tangerine are truly interchangeable, but commercially "tangerine" refers to the variety with a red-orange skin. The flavor is quite similar, regardless. Noted liqueurs with mandarin flavoring include Mandarin Napoleon from France and Van der Hum from South Africa.
