lemon shrub

Also known as lemon sherbet

Lemon shrub is a homemade aromatic preparation of lemon oleo-saccharum dissolved in lemon juice. To produce, macerate lemon zest in granulated sugar four hours to extract the essential oils, then add lemon juice, stir to dissolve and strain out the zest. The ratio of lemon juice to oleo-saccharum is to-taste, but will be (more-or-less) the amount of juice extracted from pressing the fruit zested to make the oleo-saccharum in the first place.
