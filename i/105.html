101 Best New Cocktails

flowers

Flowers is the generic category for a fresh flower garnish, most often seen floating on the surfaces of tropical punch drinks. The flower is intended not only to impart beauty, but scent as well, and such drinks are best sipped nose-up-to-flower instead of through a straw. The Vanda Orchid is the variety traditionally used in Hawaiian leis. The gardenia is notable in the Scorpion.
