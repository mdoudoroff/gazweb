dark créme de cacao

Dark crème de cacao is generic for liqueur with chocolate flavoring and caramel coloring in a neutral spirits base. Virtually all liqueur companies with broad product ranges produce a crème de cacao, usually in both white (clear) & brown-colored versions. Other than the coloration, the products are usually identical.
