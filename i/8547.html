Dirty Sue Dirty Martini Mix

Dirty Sue Dirty Martini Mix is a proprietary example of olive brine.

Olive brine is the bottled preservative of water and salt in which marinated green cocktail olives reside. The brine, having taken on some of the flavor of that which it is preserving, is used as an additive itself in some drinks, notably the Dirty Martini.
