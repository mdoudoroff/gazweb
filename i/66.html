spanish brandy

Also known as Brandy de Jerez

Spanish brandy is generic for the grape brandies of Jerez, Spain, the same region responsible for sherry. Some Spanish brandy is dry, most is sweet. All are aged in American oak casks that were previously used for aging sherry.
