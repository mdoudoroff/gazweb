Smith & Cross

Proprietary, heavy Jamaican pot still rum bottled at “Navy Strength”, probably caramel-colored. Combines unaged and briefly-aged rums. Loaded with funk and flavor.

Jamaican rum is generic for the heavy-bodied, rich, flavorful rums fermented, distilled, and aged in Jamaica. Note: except for the unaged white overproof rums popular with the natives (and relatively uncommon in cocktails), Jamaican rum is typically aged, even if it is subsequently stripped of its color through filtering. If a recipe calls for “aged Jamaican rum”, go for quality, not color.
