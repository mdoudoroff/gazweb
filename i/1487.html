Domaine de Canton

Also known as Canton ginger liqueur

Proprietary ginger liqueur with a moderately sharp, peppery ginger character with a brandy base. Replaced the softer Original Canton Delicate Ginger Liqueur in the marketplace.

Ginger-flavored liqueur is a generic category for liqueurs with a neutral spirit or brandy base and natural or artificial ginger flavoring.
