Tio Pepe Palomino Fino Sherry

Also known as Tio Pepe Fino Sherry

Noted proprietary brand of fino sherry.

Fino sherry is one of the principal styles of dry sherry (the other being oloroso). Unlike oloroso, fino sherry is unoxidized. Fino is barrel aged in a manner to inhibit oxidation. Once a bottle of fino sherry is opened, it should be consumed quickly; half bottles recommended.
