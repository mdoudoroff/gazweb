Stirrings Blood Orange Bitters

Also known as blood orange bitters

Proprietary bitter blood orange syrup. Although marketed as “bitters”, this product is actually a non-alcoholic mixer.

Syrup is a generic category for a thick, high-concentration water and sugar solution, either flavored or unflavored used to sweeten drinks. Also used to refer to sweetened concentrate of fruit juice used to sweeten and flavor drinks.
