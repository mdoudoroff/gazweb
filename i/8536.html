sandalwood oil

Sandalwood oil is a fragrant, distilled extract from the tree of the same name, common for use in perfuming.

Tinctures & extracts is an abstract category for concentrated flavors in an alcohol or oil base. In cocktails, an alcoholic extract of an herb or spice, typically produced by infusing neutral spirits with an herbs or spices for a period of weeks. Tinctures are used in minute quantities to layer concentrated flavors into drinks. Tinctures are also used in producing liqueurs (often of poor quality).
