Chikurin Hou Hou Shu “Bubble Bubble Sake”

Proprietary sparkling sake.

Sparkling sake is a generic category for Japanese or Japanese-style wines made from rice, spring or mountain water, yeast, and a fungus known as Koji mold. The distinction is that sparkling sake is given a secondary fermentation that carbonates it.
