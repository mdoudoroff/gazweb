pineapple syrup

Also known as sirop de ananas

Pineapple syrup is a homemade or manufactured nonalcoholic sugar syrup flavored with pineapple juice or extract and/or artificial flavorings. Known as a punch ingredient, in particular for the Pisco Punch.
