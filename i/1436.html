cinnamon-infused sugar syrup

Also known as cinnamon syrup, Don’s Spices #4

Cinnamon-infused sugar syrup is a home-made preparation employed in tropical drinks. Make as follows:

Crush 3 cinnamon sticks and place in a saucepan with 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water. Bring to a boil, stirring until sugar is dis-solved. Lower heat, cover saucepan, and simmer for 2 minutes. Remove saucepan from heat and, keeping it covered, let sit at least 2 hours before straining and bottling. It should keep about a month in the fridge.

Note: if you’re making Don the Beachcomber drink that calls for “Don’s Spices #4”, it’s cinnamon syrup.
