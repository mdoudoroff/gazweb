Giffard Lichi Li

Giffard Lichi Li is a proprietary example of lychee liqueur.

Lychee liqueur is liqueur flavored with the fruit of a tropical evergreen tree of the same name, usually in a neutral spirits base. The lychee flavor is “fragrant” in a floral sense, a bit pungent, and sweet.
