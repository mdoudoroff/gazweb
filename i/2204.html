Havana Club Añejo 3 Años

Also known as Havana Club 3 year

Straw-colored three-year-old rum distilled from fermented molasses and aged in used whiskey barrels.

Light Cuban rum is generic for the light-bodied aged rums of Cuba stripped of all or most of their color through filtration and epitomized by the storied-but-now-extinct “Carta Blanca” Bacardi rums prior to the Cuban Revolution
