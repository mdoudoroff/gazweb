Mumm Cordon Rouge

Mumm Cordon Rouge is a proprietary example of brut champagne.

Brut Champagne is generic for the driest and most common style of Champagne, with less than 3g sugar per liter. (This is sugar that is added—the “dosage”—following bottle fermentation.)
