lapsang souchong syrup

Lapsang souchong syrup is generic for (typically) homemade simple syrup infused with lapsang souchong (smoky black) tea. To produce, infuse 3 grams lapsang souchong per 6 oz of boiling water for five minutes, strain, and stir in an equal amount of sugar. Cool, bottle and keep refrigerated.
