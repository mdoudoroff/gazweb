Booker’s Bourbon

Also known as Fred Noe Select

Proprietary bourbon produced by Jim Beam Distillery. Note that “Fred Noe Select” is a bottling for the Japanese Market with Booker Noe’s face on the label. ABV varies a little from batch to batch; consult bottle for details.

Bourbon whiskey is a generic category for U.S. single-malt whiskey distilled from predominately corn mash with wheat or rye grains. Popular in all manner of mixed drinks 19th century-present.
