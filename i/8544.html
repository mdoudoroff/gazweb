Joseph Cartron Banana

Joseph Cartron Banana is a proprietary example of banana liqueur.

Banana liqueur is a generic category for any of a number of varieties of liqueur flavored with any of several types of banana in either neutral spirit, brandy, or rum base. Coloring also varies from yellow to amber to green. Most often styled as “creme de banane” or “creme de banana”.
