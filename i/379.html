vanilla beans

Vanilla bean is a bean pod, the flavor of which is used in individual drinks, punches, bottled liqueurs, and spirits such as some rums in bean, extract, and syrup, as well as spirit & liqueur constituent forms. The pod itself and/or the tiny seeds within can be used for flavoring (when infused) and as a decadent garnish. Madagascar is the main supplier of vanilla beans.

OriginMadagascar
