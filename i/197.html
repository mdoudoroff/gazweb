white crème de menthe

Also known as creme de mint, menthe

Crème de menthe is a generic category for liqueur with spearmint flavoring in a neutral spirit base. Virtually all liqueur companies with broad product ranges produce a crème de menthe, usually in both white (clear) & green-colored versions. Other than the coloration, the products are usually identical.

SubstituteGet 31 or Branca Menta
