Monin Vanilla Syrup

Also known as Monin Vanille

Monin Vanilla Syrup is a proprietary example of vanilla syrup.

Vanilla syrup is generic for a sugar syrup infused with vanilla flavoring, either commercial or homemade.

Homemade vanilla syrup is trivial to produce: simply create a simple syrup and add vanilla extract. The proportions will depend on the strength of the extract you're using, but you could start with 1 cup simple syrup and a scant 1 tsp vanilla extract and adjust from there. Using a demerara sugar or a portion of brown sugar will add additional flavor and color. For deluxe vanilla syrup, infuse a split vanilla bean in the syrup. Various recipes are available on the internet.
