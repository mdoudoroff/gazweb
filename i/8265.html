Westford Hill Distillers Pear William

Westford Hill Distillers Pear William is a proprietary example of poire williams.

Poire Williams is generic for French pear eau de vie, distilled from Williams pears, and of some fame. Strong, and strongly-flavored. Often produced in a signature style whereby a live pear is grown in its bottle and filled with the distillate thereafter.
