Tequila Ocho Blanco

Also known as Tequila Ocho Plata

White tequila unusual for carrying a vintage (year of harvest and distillation) as well as the estate of harvest, which may vary from year to year.

Tequila blanco is a generic category for colorless and near-colorless tequila distilled from 100% fermented agave and aged no more than two months.

OriginJalisco
