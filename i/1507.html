Cruzan Blackstrap Rum

Also known as blackstrap rum

Proprietary dark rum flavored with blackstrap molasses and featuring a uniquely strong molasses flavor. Aged two years. Copiously darkened with caramel coloring. Basically a unique product for adding color and molasses aromatics to mixed drinks.

Flavored rum is an abstract category for rums flavored with natural ingredients or artificial flavors. Flavored rums range from home-made and artisanal products intended to harmonize and marry the character of a particular rum style with other desirable flavors to products that are little more than flavored vodka.
