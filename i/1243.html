green crème de menthe

Green crème de menthe is a generic category for liqueur with spearmint flavoring in a neutral spirit base plus added natural or artificial green coloring. Virtually all liqueur companies with broad product ranges produce a crème de menthe, usually in both white (clear) & green-colored versions. Other than the coloration, the products are usually identical.

Substitutehomemade combination of white creme de menthe and green food coloring or Get 27.
