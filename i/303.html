cranberry juice

Cranberry juice is the bright, tart red juice (commercially extracted) of cranberries, often sold in blends with other, less-concentrated fruit juices, such as apple. Cranberry juice is most noted as an ingredient of the Cosmopolitan Cocktail (c. 1980s-1990s).
