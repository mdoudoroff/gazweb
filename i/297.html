coconut cream

Also known as creme of coconut

Coconut cream is a thick liquid produced by steeping and squeezing the grated coconut meat in hot water or scalded milk. The result is refrigerated and separates. The top layer is the cream which is sweetened when produced for use in mixed drinks, though is also available in minimally sweetened form. Very prevalent as a mixer in mainly tropical drinks such as the Piña Colada.
