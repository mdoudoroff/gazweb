sotol

Sotol is a generic category of spirit distilled from the cooked, fermented heart of the Desert Spoon succulent, native to Northern Mexico, New Mexico and Texas. The flavor is comparable to mescal, but with a different vegetal profile emphasizing aloe flavors.

OriginChihuahua
