Appleton Estate Extra 12 Year Old

Also known as Appleton Estate Extra, Appleton Extra

Proprietary brown Jamaican rum aged at least twelve years in used Jack Daniels barrels.

Amber Jamaican rum is generic for the amber-hued, heavy-bodied, rich, flavorful rums fermented, distilled, and aged in Jamaica. The color of these rums owes to aging, minimal filtering and the judicious addition of caramel color for consistency.
