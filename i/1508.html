Cruzan Single Barrel Estate Rum

Proprietary blend of 5- to 12-year aged rums subsequently—and unusually—finished in new, charred American oak casks (the sort of wood employed with Bourbon and Straight Rye).

Gold Virgin Islands rum is generic for the amber rums of Virgin Islands, their color the result of aging, less filtration before bottling, and the judicious addition of caramel color for consistency.
